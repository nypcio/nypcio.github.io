Welcome

Hello, my fellow coders.
On a sunny July day, I came across a cool coding challenge called NYPC. Some questions were challenging, some were easier to approach, but most importantly, I had quite a bit of fun trying NYPC coding problems.
It would've been a shame to have fun on my own, so I decided to share some of the problems with all of you.
If you're on this website reading this paragraph, you're in luck.
Dive in and code away!

So.. what is NYPC?

Well, this is the best answer I've got...

NYPC, also known as the Nexon Youth Programming Challenge, is a major coding competition for young people in Korea.
Some of the challenge problems are based on the real games created by NEXON, so if you are familiar with the games mentioned, I'm sure you will enjoy the problems even more.
The goal is to share these problems with people outside of Korea as well, so here I am, sharing the translated questions from NYPC.
Whether you want to sharpen your programming skills, take a challenge as a beginner, or even to relieve your boredom, dive in and share my interest in these problems - trust me, it will be worth your time.
