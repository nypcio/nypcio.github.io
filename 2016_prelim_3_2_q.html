Level Up

Sometimes when we are playing a game, there are times when we modify the experience table in order to help players level up easier. In fact, at times when the maximum level possible increases, the experience table is also modified.
In such a case, in order to not affect the original characters, we need to recalculate their levels and experience scores. Let us create a program that does so.
The maximum level possible currently was 45, and the experience table was as follows:

In the case the maximum level increases to level 70, the table changes to the following:

Current Level | Experience needed to level up ------|------------ 1| 498 2 | 505 3 | 529 4 | 581 5 | 671 6 | 814 7 | 1024 8 | 1317 9 | 1711 10 | 2226 11 | 2884 12 | 3706 13 | 4718 14 | 5945 15 | 7415 16 | 9157 17 | 11202 18 | 13582 19 | 16332 20 | 19486 21 | 23083 22 | 27161 23 | 31760 24 | 36922 25 | 42691 26 | 49113 27 | 56233 28 | 64101 29 | 72766 30 | 82280 31 | 92696 32 | 104069 33 | 116455 34 | 129913 35 | 144501 36 | 160282 37 | 177318 38 | 195673 39 | 215413 40 | 236606 41 | 259322 42 | 283630 43 | 309604 44 | 337317 45 | 366845 46 | 398265 47 | 431656 48 | 467098 49 | 504674 ... | ...

Given the current character's level and the experience score it received in the current level, by calculating the total amount of experience score it has acquired so far, write a program that outputs the new level and experience score based on the modified experience table.

Input

Two integers L, X will be given. L is the currentl level and it is in between 1 and 45 inclusive. X is the current experience score within the current level, and it is always greater than or equal to 0 and less than the experience score needed to move on to the next level. If L is 45, X is always 0.

Output

Similar to the input, output the modified level and the acquired experience score with space in between.

Input Example 1

       5 0

Output Example 1

          8 368

Before changing the experience table, the acquired experience score until level 5 is 898 + 988 + 1288 + 1816 = 4990.
After the modified experience table, experience score needed to get to level 8 is 498 + 505 + 529 + 581 + 671 + 814 + 1024 = 4622. Therefore, the level changes to 8 and the experience score left is 368.

Input Example 2

       10 1823

Output Example 2

          15 5924

Input Example 3

       45 0

Output Example 3

          49 332334

Just because you were at the maximum level before the modified experience table doesn't mean you are automatically at the max level in the modified experience table. In that case, you just need to know the information until level 49.
