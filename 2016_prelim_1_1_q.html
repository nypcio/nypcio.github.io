Drink the Stamina Potion

Potion is a common item that appears in games, and drinking it produces a lot of effects. Out of all the effects, the most common is the red potion, which boosts your energy.

In the currently developing game The Legend of Tales Story 3, the stamina postion has the following rules.

Let's first say the character's current energy is A and its maximum energy is B. A and B are always positive integers. In such case, if a character drinks a potion that boosts the energy by H:
           - If A + H doesn't go over B (the maximum energy), the character's energy becomes A + H.
           - If A + H does exceed B, the exceeding amount (B - (A + H))'s 25% is boosted, giving the character the energy that exceeds the maximum energy.
           - For the case where the character's energy is boosted and it goes over the maxmimum, the exceeding amount, in the case that it is a decimal number, the numbers after the decimal point is dropped.
           - When exceeding the maxmium energy, you cannot exceed 1.5 times your original energy. Again, the numbers after the decimal point is dropped.

Given the character's current energy, maximum energy, and the amount the potion boosts your energy, write a program that determines the end energy after drinking the potion.

Input

First line of input has A, B, H with spaces in between. All input numbers are positive integers and should be less than or equal to 999. A, B, H are the current energy, maximum energy, and energy boost amount respectively. The current energy must be less than or equal to the maximum energy.

Output

Output the ending result of the character's energy after drinking the potion.

Input Example 1

        50 100 30

Output Example 1

       80

Input Example 2

        50 100 70

Output Example 2 

        105

Input Example 3

        50 100 73

Output Example 3

        105

Input Example 4

        50 100 999

Output Example 4 

        150

Even after drinking a potion with a huge energy boost, you cannot exceed 150% of your current energy. Excessive drinking of potions can lead to addiction.

Marks Guideline

Marks will be awarded according to the number of hidden test cases the program passes. If you miss even one, you will receive no marks.
